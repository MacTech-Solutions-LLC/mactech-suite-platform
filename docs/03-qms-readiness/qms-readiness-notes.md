# QMS Readiness Notes

## Purpose

This document captures early quality-management readiness notes for future
planning. It does not assert that the repository, process, or product is
certified, compliant, validated, or production-ready.

## Initial Readiness Themes

- Traceable decisions through ADRs
- Reviewable pull requests
- Evidence folders for future change, review, and test artifacts
- Security-safe editing rules
- Non-deploying automation during foundation work

## Future Topics

- Document control expectations
- Change-control workflow
- Risk management process
- Design and development traceability
- Verification and validation evidence
- Training and role-based responsibilities

Future QMS work should be reviewed with qualified stakeholders before being used
as an operating process.
